Hello! I'm Shreyash Gadgil, a robotics engineer and M.S. graduate from the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. I build intelligent systems that perceive, understand, and interact with the physical world.

My work spans robotic manipulation, autonomous navigation, mechanical design, computer vision, machine learning, and industrial automation. I have worked on systems including robotic grasp planning and semi-autonomous lithium-ion battery recycling.

Outside engineering, I enjoy mathematics, painting, sketching, philosophy, films, and volleyball. These creative interests shape how I approach research and technical problem-solving.
