## Goals

- Develop a compact tracked chassis suited to uneven terrain.
- Integrate motor drivers, power distribution, compute, and safety controls.
- Add odometry and environmental perception.
- Implement teleoperation before progressing to assisted and autonomous navigation.
- Keep the system modular so sensors and payloads can change as the project develops.
